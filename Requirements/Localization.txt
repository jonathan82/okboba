This is how the i18n Localization strings are organized:

Button_xxxxx: strings related to buttons
Nav_xxxxxxxx: strings related to navigation tabs
Section_xxxx: section header strings
Account_xxxx: strings related to Account like email, password, nickname, etc
Feed_xxxxxxx: strings related to the activity feed
Messaging_xx: strings related to the messaging module
Photo_xxxxxx: strings related to photo functions
Profile_xxxx: strings related to profile functions like questions, etc
Detail_xxxxx: strings related to the profile details
QAndA_xxxxxx: strings related to the the question and answering functions
Match_xxxxxx: strings related to matching functions
Time_xxxxxxx: strings related to time
Error_xxxxxx: error message strings
